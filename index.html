TRACK GREEN

Track Green is a Webapp created and that by the help of machine learning using tensorflow is trained in such a way to calculate the area covered by trees in a particular locality. It can then Recognise the area with greenery and can help us in several ways like:-

  • 1: The house vs tree ratio.
  • 2: The ratio of planted and unplanted land.
  • 3: At very far sight it gonna help us in knowing the pollution and carbon sink in the particular area.
