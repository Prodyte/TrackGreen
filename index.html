Welcome to TRACK GREEN

Actually, we thought to design a app/webapp by the help of machine learning and tensor flow.In our app we gonna train pur model to recognise the number of trees in a given area . Recognising the area with greenery can help us in several ways like:- 1: The house vs tree ratio. 2: The ratio of planted and unplanted land. 3: At very far sight it gonna help us in knowing the pollution and carbon sinkin the particular area.

Technologies we are using are:-

    Tensor flow
    OpenCV
    Pre-trained model
